Projects

Car Selling App (Android)

The Car Selling App is a user-friendly Android application designed to facilitate the buying and selling of cars through a mobile platform. Developed using Java and Android Studio, the app features a modern UI and includes key functionalities such as user authentication, car listings with images and specifications, search and filter options, and in-app messaging between buyers and sellers. Users can post ads, browse vehicles by brand or price, and view detailed car profiles with pricing, mileage, and contact information. Integration with Firebase enables real-time database management and secure data storage. This app offers a seamless experience for individuals and dealerships looking to sell or purchase vehicles through their smartphones.
